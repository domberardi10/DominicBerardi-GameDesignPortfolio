About Me

Hello! My name is Dominic Berardi, welcome to my portfolio. I'm an aspiring game designer. My skills have a large range, from game scripting and programming, designing levels and gameplay loops, and even dabbling in VFX and graphics a bit too. Typically on a game project I take an engineering role, but I'm flexible and always ready to learn new things or put my skills to the test. I've always wanted to create, and games are the perfect intersection of technical and artistic design that I strive to achieve when developing a project.
